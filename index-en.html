ELBA (Escuela de Lingüística de Buenos Aires) is a Linguistics school organized by graduate and undergraduate students from Argentina with the support of the Instituto de Filología y Literaturas Hispánicas "Dr. Amado Alonso" (Philology and Hispanic Literature Institute "Dr. Amado Alonso") and the Departamento de Letras (Language & Literature Department) of the University of Buenos Aires (UBA).

The School aims to promote discussion on formal Linguistics and related areas. Each edition includes courses on different topics such as General Linguistics, formal Semantics, Phonology, Syntax, Psycholinguistics or Natural Language Processing, among others. Each year, a Workshop also takes place, the day after the end of the School. This year, courses will take place from July 22 to July 25 and the Workshop will take place on July 26 at the Facultad de Filosofía y Letras of the University of Buenos Aires (Puan 480, Ciudad Autónoma de Buenos Aires).
